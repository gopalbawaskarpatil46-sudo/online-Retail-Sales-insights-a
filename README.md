# 📊 Online Retail Sales Analysis using Microsoft Excel

## Overview

This project analyzes an Online Retail dataset using **Microsoft Excel** to uncover sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, and business performance. The analysis includes data cleaning, aggregation, interactive dashboard creation, and business insight generation.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate practical data analytics skills using Excel by transforming raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision making.

---

# Business Objective

The primary objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Compare domestic and international sales.
- Evaluate country-wise sales performance.
- Analyze invoice trends.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business users.
- Generate actionable business recommendations.

---

# Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional records from an online retail business, including:

- Invoice information
- Product descriptions
- Quantity sold
- Unit price
- Customer IDs
- Countries
- Invoice dates

The analysis was performed entirely in **Microsoft Excel**.

# Product Performance

The three highest revenue-contributing products are:

1. DOTCOM POSTAGE
2. REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
3. PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE

Together, these products contribute approximately **47% of total sales revenue**, indicating a strong concentration of revenue among a small number of products.

---

# Key Business Insights

### 1. Strong Overall Business Performance

The business generated **$10.6 million** in revenue across **19,960 invoices**, serving customers in **38 countries**.

---

### 2. Sales Peak During September to November

Sales increase significantly between **September and November**, suggesting strong seasonal demand during this period.

---

### 3. Heavy Dependence on Domestic Market

Approximately:

- Domestic Sales: **90%**
- International Sales: **10%**

This indicates that the business relies heavily on its domestic market.

---

### 4. Revenue Concentration

Nearly half of total revenue comes from only three products, highlighting both their importance and the potential business risk of relying heavily on a limited product range.

---

### 5. Global Customer Reach

Although domestic sales dominate, the business successfully serves customers across **38 countries**, demonstrating an established international presence.

---

### 6. December 2011 Data Consideration

Sales appear to decline during **December 2011**. However, this should **not be interpreted as an actual decrease in business performance**, as the available December data may be incomplete.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Expand international sales to reduce dependence on the domestic market.
- Diversify the product portfolio to reduce revenue concentration risk.
- Prepare inventory and marketing campaigns ahead of the September to November peak season.
- Continue monitoring customer purchasing trends to identify additional high-performing products.
- Use dashboard insights to support regular business performance reviews.
